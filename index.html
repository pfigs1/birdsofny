There's so much to see here in New York City so it's easy to overlook the amazing wildlife population. The birds that fly the skies and walk these streets are colorful, diverse and numerous; much like their human neighbors.
From the iconic pigeon to the noisy coastal seagull, we have highlighted a few of our favorites on this page. Browse our gallery of images, check out a local bird-watching event and get to know our feathered New Yorkers.

We hope you visit often and enjoy the content.
